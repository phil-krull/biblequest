Bible Quest

Bible Quest empowers parents to teach their children to use God's Word as the FOUNDATION for their thought process! Here's how:

  • We EQUIP all learners with knowledge through memorization.
    • The Big Bible Story Song makes Biblical chronology into an easy, memorized reference.
    • Simple facts about key Bible personalities help kids know who God was working with.
    • Easy-to-remember verse songs with the included CD keeps God’s Word in kids' memories.
    • Maps of important places in the Bible help reinforce the historical reality of the Biblical accounts.
  • We EMPOWER older students with Explorations and Exercises to empower their Biblical understanding.
  • EXPEDITIONS and deeper questions bring intense discussion and wisdom development for teens and advanced learners.
  • Two, separate 40-Week editions (Old Testament and New Testament) provide a total of two years of material.
The Equip Phase builds biblical knowledge through memorization. Maximize and master memorized content with active, visual, and audio elements!
The Empower Phase aims to develop student understanding through Explorations and Exercises. Explorations involve questions and activities that help students think Biblically. Exercises provide practice using Biblical information with speed and accuracy.
Expeditions help students of the Bible to use what they know and understand to communicate biblical truth to others. By engaging with Biblical truth under the guidance of a mentor, students develop biblical wisdom.
